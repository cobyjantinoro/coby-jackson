Where to Eat in Miami

A Cultural Food Experience

Miami offers a vast array of numerous cuisines that are constantly making headlines in popular food publications. Due to it's rich culture and hospitality centric environment, you'll always find somthing delicious in Miami. When narrowing down the best for your limited time in town, the top restaurants to visit while during your stay are Seaspice in South Beach, Casa Tua Cucina in Brickell, and 1-800 Lucky in Wynwood. All three restaurants provide mouth-watering dishes in unique locations and with elegant interior design and overall great atmospheres. Seapsice is a hip spot near the water with an outdoor patio, globally influenced seafood an additional hot-stone & crudo lounge. Meanwhile, Casa Tua is upscale food hall in the Brickell City Center offering Italian pasta, pizza, salads, crudos & sweets, plus coffee & a bar. 1-800 Lucky is also a spot you need to check out, the expansive food hall and outdoor area features a range of Asian-inspired cuisine in industrial digs.
